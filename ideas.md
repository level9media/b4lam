<idea>

**Design Movement:** Brutalist Neon Noir
**Core Principles:**
1. Raw, unapologetic typography — oversized, clipped, bleeding off edges
2. High-contrast black-and-crimson with electric red neon accents
3. Asymmetric layouts that feel like torn magazine pages
4. Grit and grain textures layered over everything

**Color Philosophy:** Pure black (#000) as the void, crimson red (#FF0022) as the pulse of energy — danger, power, urgency. No softness. No gradients except neon bleeds.

**Layout Paradigm:** Full-bleed asymmetric columns. Text runs vertically on the left margin. Content blocks are offset, overlapping, intentionally misaligned to create tension.

**Signature Elements:**
1. Horizontal scan-line overlays (CRT monitor effect)
2. Glitch text animations on headings
3. Red neon underlines that "flicker" on hover

**Interaction Philosophy:** Every hover triggers a micro-glitch. Clicks feel like circuit breaks — sharp, immediate, no easing.

**Animation:** Glitch keyframes on hero text. Scan-line sweep on page load. Neon flicker (opacity 0.8→1→0.9→1) on accent elements.

**Typography System:** "Bebas Neue" for display (all caps, tracked wide) + "Space Mono" for body (monospace, technical feel)

**Design Movement:** Dark Luxury Cyber
**Core Principles:**
1. Deep obsidian backgrounds with razor-thin gold and red accent lines
2. Cinematic proportions — everything feels like a film title sequence
3. Minimal text, maximum visual weight
4. Spatial hierarchy through light, not color

**Color Philosophy:** Near-black (#0A0A0A) base. Muted gold (#C9A84C) for prestige. Neon red (#FF1A1A) for urgency. The combination reads as "elite agency that operates in the shadows."

**Layout Paradigm:** Full-viewport sections with centered typographic anchors. Content reveals on scroll through clip-path wipes.

**Signature Elements:**
1. Thin horizontal rule lines that animate in from left
2. Masked text reveals (clip-path expanding on scroll)
3. Subtle particle field in hero background

**Interaction Philosophy:** Deliberate, slow, weighty. Hover states expand with 400ms ease-out. Nothing feels cheap or fast.

**Animation:** Clip-path text reveals. Slow parallax on background layers. Fade-up on section entries with 60ms stagger.

**Typography System:** "Cormorant Garamond" italic for display (unexpected elegance) + "DM Sans" for body (clean, modern contrast)

**Design Movement:** Cyberpunk Signal — CHOSEN
**Core Principles:**
1. Black-dominant canvas with electric red (#FF0033) as the primary neon signal color
2. Layered depth: dark backgrounds, mid-layer noise/grain, foreground neon glow text
3. Motion as meaning — animations communicate energy and forward momentum
4. Industrial precision: sharp edges, no rounded corners, grid-locked structure

**Color Philosophy:** Black (#000000) is the foundation — total darkness. Electric red (#FF0033) is the brand signal — danger, power, dominance. Muted charcoal (#111111, #1A1A1A) for card surfaces. White (#FFFFFF) used sparingly for maximum contrast impact.

**Layout Paradigm:** Full-bleed sections with left-anchored content blocks. Navigation is minimal and fixed. Hero sections use full-viewport height with layered z-index depth (video → grain overlay → neon text).

**Signature Elements:**
1. Neon glow text-shadow on all major headings (red glow, multi-layer)
2. Animated scan-line / noise grain overlay across the entire site
3. Sharp rectangular dividers (no curves) with red accent lines

**Interaction Philosophy:** Fast, electric, immediate. Hover states trigger neon intensification. Buttons have a "power surge" effect on hover — border glow expands outward.

**Animation:** Splash screen: logo fades in with neon pulse. Hover: background image fades in at 60% opacity. Click: cinematic wipe transition to main site. Hero: video plays behind grain overlay. Headings: neon flicker on load.

**Typography System:** "Barlow Condensed" ExtraBold for display (industrial, condensed, powerful) + "Barlow" Regular for body (cohesive family, clean readability)

</idea>

## Selected Direction: Cyberpunk Signal

Dark, electric, cinematic. Black canvas + neon red. Industrial typography. Layered depth with grain overlays. Every interaction feels like a power surge.
